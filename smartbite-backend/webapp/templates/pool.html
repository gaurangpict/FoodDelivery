How to Demo ALNS Batching

  1. Open this app in two browser tabs (or use a normal + incognito window)
  2. In Tab 1: Order from a restaurant (e.g., Pizzeria Downtown on FC Road) → Standard delivery → Place Order
  3. The order enters the pool (visible on this page)
  4. In Tab 2: Order from a nearby restaurant (e.g., Dragon Wok in Shivajinagar, ~1km away) → Standard delivery → Place Order
  5. ALNS validates the combined route — if fuel savings ≥ 10% and delay ≤ 15 min, both orders are batched with a ₹15 delivery fee + discount!

Pool timeout: 2 minutes. ALNS threshold: ≥10% fuel savings, ≤15 min delay, ≤0.5 km delivery radius.
